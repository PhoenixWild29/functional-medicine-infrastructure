# CompoundIQ Launch Kit

Working documents for the first 30 days of go-to-market execution. These are **not** investor-facing polished docs — they're the templates and checklists you'll actively use to lock in design partners and start generating revenue.

## Contents

| File | Purpose | When to Use |
|------|---------|-------------|
| [clinic-outreach-email.md](clinic-outreach-email.md) | Email templates for warm clinic introductions | Week 1 — send to 3-5 warm contacts |
| [pharmacy-outreach-email.md](pharmacy-outreach-email.md) | Email templates for compounding pharmacies | Week 2-3 — **AFTER** you have a committed clinic to name-drop |
| [legitscript-application-checklist.md](legitscript-application-checklist.md) | Step-by-step LegitScript certification guide | Week 1 — submit in parallel; 2-4 week turnaround |
| [design-partner-agreement.md](design-partner-agreement.md) | Non-binding Letter of Intent template | Week 1-2 — send after a clinic verbally commits |

## The Sequence

```
Week 1:  Clinic outreach (5-8 warm contacts)  +  LegitScript application submitted
         ↓
Week 2:  1-2 clinics verbally committed        → send Design Partner Agreement (LOI)
         ↓
Week 3:  Clinic LOI signed                     → pharmacy outreach with clinic name-drop
         ↓
Week 4:  Pharmacy catalog received             → load into app via /ops/catalog upload
         ↓
         First real prescription end-to-end    → your first $22.50 platform fee
```

## Key Principle

**Clinic commitment unlocks everything downstream.** Pharmacy outreach cold is weak. Pharmacy outreach with a committed clinic name attached converts at ~40% vs ~5% cold. Do not skip the order.
