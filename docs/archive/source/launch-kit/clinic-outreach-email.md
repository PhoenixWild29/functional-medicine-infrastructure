# Clinic Outreach Email Templates

## Principles

1. **Short.** Under 150 words. Busy clinicians skim.
2. **Personal.** Mention something specific to them — their practice, a conversation you had, a pain point they mentioned.
3. **Concrete ask.** "15-minute demo this week" — not "can we chat sometime?"
4. **Easy path forward.** Include a Calendly link or offer 2-3 specific times.
5. **Let the product speak.** Link to the live app + 2-page quickstart PDF. Let them self-serve if they want.

## Follow-Up Sequence (Use With Any Template)

**If no response in 4 business days — send Follow-Up 1:**

> Hi [FIRST NAME] — just floating this back up in case it got buried. 15 minutes this week? Happy to work around your schedule.

**If no response in 7 days after Follow-Up 1 — send Follow-Up 2:**

> Last one from me on this — wanted to make sure you have the demo link so you can explore on your own time: https://functional-medicine-infrastructure.vercel.app (I'll DM the login if useful). Door's open whenever.

**If no response after Follow-Up 2:** drop it. Move on. Circle back in 60 days if you have new product news.

## Quick Sanity Checks Before Sending

- [ ] First name spelled right? (Double check — spelling their name wrong kills the email)
- [ ] Subject line under 60 characters? (Mobile preview truncation)
- [ ] Specific pain point or mutual connection referenced? (Proves it's not a blast)
- [ ] 3 specific times offered OR Calendly link? (Don't say "whenever works")
- [ ] Demo URL + login included? (Let them self-serve)
- [ ] Your signature has phone number? (Some people prefer to just call)
- [ ] Sent from YOUR email, not a generic noreply@? (Trust factor)
